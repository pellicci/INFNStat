
# Exercises for the INFN School of Statistics 2026

The goal of these exercises is to get acquainted with few of the statistical analysis tools (mostly used in HEP). Introductory slides will present a general introduction on the main features of RooFit and RooStats, and will give some additional information on the hands-on session. 

 These exercises use the public Run-1 data from CMS at the LHC, corresponding to about 25 fb-1 of luminosity collected between 2011 and 2012. The purpose is to "discover" the Higgs boson in the 4-lepton final state and characterize its properties.

Our observable is the 4-lepton invariant mass spectrum we will use for most of the exercises:
